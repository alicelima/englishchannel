    Why to learn English

    There are many reasons why learning a new language is a good idea. It allows you to communicate with new people. It helps you to see things from a different perspective, or get a deeper understanding of another culture. It even has health benefits, as studies have shown that people who speak two or more languages have more active minds later in life!

    Movies to learn English

    The Wizard of Oz (1939)

    The story follows a girl called Dorothy, transported to a magical world which she’s destined to save.

    Jurassic Park (1993)

    It’s the perfect movie for learning some slightly complicated scientific words in a thoroughly entertaining context.

    Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

    It's a universally amusing film– for the young and old its fun themes, outlandish costumes and set designs could keep you engaged from beginning to end.
